My Design Process

1

Research & Discovery

I begin by understanding the problem space through user research, stakeholder interviews, and competitive analysis. This phase establishes a solid foundation for the design process.

2

Ideation & Wireframing

Translating research insights into low-fidelity wireframes and user flows. This stage focuses on information architecture and core functionality before visual design.

3

UI Design & Prototyping

Creating high-fidelity designs with attention to visual hierarchy, typography, color theory, and accessibility. Interactive prototypes bring the design to life.

4

Testing & Iteration

Conducting usability testing to validate design decisions and identify areas for improvement. Iterative refinement based on user feedback ensures the final product meets user needs.
